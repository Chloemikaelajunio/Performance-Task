Access To Treatments

This issue occurred for a long time but it became even relevant to the majority of the countries today, mainly due to poverty and is also worsen due to the sudden emergence of the pandemic.


Epidemic Preparednesss

Due to the lack of awareness and implementation of emergencies, this issue had aggravated the world on its peak. In the context of health care and public health practices, medical interventions requires different steps to prevent and eliminate the transmission of infectious diseases, however, due to delayed actions, majority of the countries are under torment.


Capitalizing On Technological Advancements

The use of digital technologies in the Philippines, however, is still below its potential, with the country’s digital adoption generally trailing behind many regional neighbors. The “digital divide” between those with and without internet leads to unequal access to social services and life-changing economic opportunities. This pandemic has caused substantial disruptions in the domestic economy as community restrictions have limited movement of people and reduced business operations nationwide.




Health Care Sanitation

This issue transpired years ago but is currently rampant world wide due to the Covid-19 outbreak that caused insufficient worker's to hospitals and health care facilities leading to the decline of sanitary measures.
